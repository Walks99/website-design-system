Website Design System

Walks99 - Vol. 1

A reference guide

As I embark on my journey of learning to code, I understand the significance of developing my own website design system. It's an essential step in honing my skills and establishing a strong foundation for my projects. Creating a design system allows me to define my unique visual identity and ensure consistency across all my web creations. By crafting reusable components, selecting typography, and curating a color palette, I lay the groundwork for cohesive and harmonious designs. This not only saves me time and effort in the long run but also instills good design practices from the outset. As I learn to code, my design system becomes an invaluable resource, guiding me in creating intuitive layouts and delightful user experiences. It serves as a reference and a reminder of my preferred design choices, empowering me to iterate and refine my work. With each project, my design system evolves, growing more refined and reflective of my developing coding skills. Through the process of building my own design system, I not only sharpen my coding abilities but also nurture my creativity and design thinking. It's an exciting and rewarding journey that allows me to craft visually appealing, user-centered websites that truly represent my unique style and vision.
